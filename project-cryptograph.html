Project Overview

Cryptograph - Broadcast of new news and interesting publications from the world of cryptocurrencies. Getting users up-to-date opinions, events, analytics every day. This information will allow users to navigate the world of investments and cryptocurrencies.

Logotype.

The logo represents the globe and users around the world. News availability regardless of the user's location. The logo is made using smooth shapes and smooth lines, as well as a modern gradient.

Stage of work

User Research

I conducted quantitative and qualitative analyses. I also conducted and analyzed competitive research. Most often, representatives of the crypto community are interested in financial and investment services, as well as software development.

Education:

More than half of the respondents (58.2%) have a higher education, more than a quarter (26%) have a secondary vocational education. Another 12.5% of respondents have secondary specialized education. 3.3% of users have an advanced degree. 8.3% are still only studying.

Field of activity:

13.2% of respondents do not work anywhere, and 17.7% own their own business. More than half of the respondents (57.2%) are employees. Another 3.6% of survey participants did not indicate their occupation.

Income level:

50.8% of respondents called their income level average. Another 29.6% considered his income low. Above average income was 7.2% of respondents. 1.7% of users reported high income levels.
