5 Minute Gourmet

Fully responsive
e-commerce website

Project duration:
February 2025

My role:
UX Designer, UI Designer.

Responsibilities:
Creating wireframes and prototypes, Designing a responsive user interface, Visual design and brand identity, Conducting usability testing.

Tools:
Figma, Illustrator, Photoshop, Firefly

Project Overview

5 Minute Gourmetis an e-commerce website specializing in premium-quality frozen meals prepared by world-class chefs. The goal of the project was to design a visually appealing and intuitive interface that provides a smooth and convenient shopping experience across all devices.

Challenge.

The existing website lacked visual appeal and clarity, leading to low conversion rates and poor mobile performance.

Goal.

To redesign the e-commerce experience to better reflect the premium nature of the product and to improve user engagement and sales.

Target audience.

Busy professionals and food enthusiasts looking for gourmet-quality meals that are quick and easy to prepare at home.

Design features.

  • Focus on large product visuals to highlight food quality
  • Mobile-first design with thumb-friendly navigation
